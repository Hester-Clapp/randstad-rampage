# Premise
The playing area has been divided into regions. The aim of the game is to have claimed the most regions by the end of the game. Regions can be claimed by:
- Being the first team to enter the region
- Being the first team to complete the challenge in the region

All teams start at Delft Station at 9:00. The game finishes at 18:00.

Teams may only travel by public transport; this is defined as anything which follows a pre-determined route/schedule and is shared with other users. Trains, trams, metros, buses and ferries are allowed. Bikes (including OV-fiets), scooters and taxis (including ride share and water taxis) are not allowed.

To cover the cost of buses, trams and metro in Den Haag and Rotterdam, players should by a HTM Dagkaart (€8,60) and RET Dagkaart (€12,00) through the 9292 app. Trains and EBS buses can be paid for using OV-pay.

### Entering Regions
Regions have boundaries, defined by gemeente (municipality) boundaries and stadsdelen (city districts) within Den Haag/Rotterdam. If a region is unclaimed and you are the first team to cross the boundary into the region, then your team claims it.

If two teams enter a region on the same vehicle, then they are considered to enter the region simultaneously. This means neither team claims the region, and other teams cannot claim it by entering either. If two teams enter simultaneously and one of them subsequently leaves before the region's challenge has been completed, then the other team claims the region.

Since Delft is the starting point of the game, it cannot be claimed by entering, even if you are the last team to leave it.

### Doing Challenges
In order to do a challenge, your team must travel to the point of interest in the region. This is usually a town hall or another key landmark. The specified stations are the closest to these points of interest, but they are only suggestions and do not have to be used.

After reaching the point of interest, you can start the challenge. These are self-descriptive and have a possibility of failure. You are not allowed to plan or prepare for challenges before starting them.

All challenges have a specified time limit - the challenge must be completed within the time limit in order to claim the region. The timer should be started immediately after you finish reading the challenge.

You are not allowed to use public transport for the duration of the challenge.

If you succeed at completing the challenge, then your team will claim the region, even if it has already been claimed by entering. If you fail the challenge, you do not claim the region and cannot re-attempt it.

Once a team has completed the challenge for a region, no other teams can claim the region.
